Our Story

From Maun, with intent.

Built by Africans, for Africans. A safari brand that finally belongs to the continent it's named after.

Manifesto

We make clothes for people who rise early, dream wide, and walk far.

For too long, "safari clothing" was designed in distant cities for visitors passing through. WEAR THE RISE was started to change that. We're a Maun-based atelier crafting wardrobe pieces for the people who already live the adventure — guides, photographers, builders, dreamers, and anyone who refuses to dress small. Our pieces are honest. Our prices are fair. Our materials are picked to last. And every garment is finished by Motswana hands.

What we stand for

Three things, always.

01 — Durability

Built for years

Real fabrics, real stitching, real hardware. We'd rather make fewer pieces that you keep for a decade than a closet you replace each season.

02 — Freedom

Cut to move

Every piece is patterned for movement — for crouching, climbing, walking, and the kind of long-day comfort the bush demands of you.

03 — The Rise Mindset

Dress with intent

What you wear tells the world how seriously you take your day. We make clothes for people who choose to rise — and want their wardrobe to keep up.

The Founders

The hands behind the brand.

Bosele Limited Holdings is a family-led company based in Maun, Botswana. WEAR THE RISE is its first brand.

CEO & Founder

MR Topo Obonye

Born and raised at the edge of the Okavango, Topo grew up watching guides, builders, and travellers move through Maun in clothes that never quite fit the place. WEAR THE RISE is his answer — a brand designed in Botswana for the people who already call it home.

Co-Owner & Creative Director

MR Thabiso Keitiile

MR Keitiile leads design and brand. With a background in textile work and a sharp eye for honest materials, she shapes every collection — choosing every fabric, every cut, and every stitch that ends up on a customer's back.
