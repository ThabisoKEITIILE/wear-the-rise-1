The Brand

A wardrobe for the African adventure.

WEAR THE RISE is a Botswana-grown clothing house designing safari-ready apparel for a new generation of African explorers. Every garment is cut from honest, hard-wearing fabrics — linen, organic cotton, brushed canvas — and finished with details that work as hard as you do. From dawn river crossings on the Okavango to long city evenings in Gaborone, our pieces are built to rise with you.
