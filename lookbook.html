Dawn on the Delta

Linen shirt, rolled twice at the cuff. Cargo trouser. Bush hat. Pulled together with the canvas tote and a pair of broken-in boots. The whole outfit moves quietly — exactly what you want when the river is still glass and the elephants are starting to wake.

Look 02

Long days under high sun

Bush tee with the walking short and explorer cap. Built for the kind of day that starts at five and ends at the fire. Layer the overshirt for the cool that comes after sundown — Botswana evenings turn quickly.

Pieces: Acacia Bush Tee · Savanna Walking Short · Explorer Cap · Bush Camp Overshirt.

How to wear WEAR THE RISE

Layer earth tones. Sand on dust on ochre. Avoid bright synthetics that fight the landscape — let the land set the palette.

Roll the cuffs. Two turns on the linen shirt and the trousers — every time. It softens the silhouette and shows you actually use the pieces.

One hero piece. Pick a single statement — the bush hat, the canvas tote, the overshirt — and let everything else support it.

Don't fight the climate. Linen and cotton, always. Wool only after sundown. Your wardrobe should let the day in, not lock it out.
